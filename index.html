ABOUT

Vidita Bhagat

"I understand problems through people — then make whatever needs to be made to solve them."

I've spent the last four years working across early-stage startups and large enterprises; designing products, rebuilding brands, and leading research that shaped what got built next. I'm drawn to places where things move fast, the brief is still forming, and the decisions you make in week one matter. That's where I do my best work.

When I'm not delving into , you'll find me completely immersed in the vibrant world of art. Whether I'm drawing, painting, crafting, visiting art galleries, or simply appreciating the beauty around me, I find endless inspiration and joy because art truly fuels my creativity.
