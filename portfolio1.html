Project Context

Until recently, the shared mobility market was largely untapped all across the globe when suddenly, it began to grow rapidly, especially in the form of two-wheeler electric vehicle (EV) rental services. Recognizing the importance of electrifying vehicle fleets in advancing this sector, a UK-based startup saw an opportunity.

The client wanted to explore the Indian market, aiming to introduce two-wheeler EV rentals and thus, it became important to understand the market's readiness and feasibility for such a venture. To achieve this, they embarked on a research project to gather insights and evaluate the potential for success.

The goal was to provide a roadmap to enter the Indian market and seize opportunities in the burgeoning two-wheeler EV rental space. This research would pave the way for establishing successful businesses, bridging the gap between innovative ideas and practical implementation in the dynamic landscape of India's shared mobility sector.
