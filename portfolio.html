About Me

I am passionate about understanding the intricacies of human needs and behaviors that shape their existence. My academic background in psychology equips me with profound insights into human behavior, cognition, and emotions, making me a contributor to crafting strategies for user-centric designs and informing business decisions.

I thrive in research, enjoying both solitary problem-solving and collaborative work with researchers, designers, and product teams. Finding fulfillment in connecting with users while providing valuable insights that are intuitive and data-informed makes me feel like a detective (mystery being my most favourite genre), uncovering hidden truths in a vast labyrinth where each project is an exciting investigation, challenging yet immensely rewarding :)

When I'm not delving into research, you'll find me completely immersed in the vibrant world of art. Whether I'm drawing, painting, crafting, visiting art galleries, or simply appreciating the beauty around me, I find endless inspiration and joy because art truly fuels my creativity.
